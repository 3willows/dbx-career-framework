Core Responsibilities

Dropboxers in Tokyo can find a Japanese version here

Results

Own and deliver work that matters
Responsibility
Key Behaviors
Impact
I deliver the most effective results for Dropbox
  • I act with urgency and deliver high-quality work that will add the most value
  • I get work to a simple place by focusing on the heart of the problem and prioritizing the right things
  • I redirect my focus if my results aren’t moving the needle for our goals or serving the needs of customers in a meaningful way
Ownership
I’m accountable for my results
  • I follow through on my commitments and take responsibility for my / my team’s work
  • I own my failures and learn from them
  • I unblock myself and others by removing barriers and focusing on solutions
Decision-making
I make informed and timely decisions
  • I make informed decisions by consulting the right stakeholders and balancing details with the big picture
  • I think critically and make timely decisions without sacrificing quality or trust
Operational Excellence (Manager only)
I lead my team with rigor and efficiency
  • I set goals and objectives at the right altitude and hold my team accountable to them
  • I run efficient processes, while minimizing unnecessary work about work
  • I drive operational cadences that get to the right outcomes

Direction

Think big, take risks, and inspire others
Responsibility
Key Behaviors 
Agility
I embrace change and can adapt
  • I’m able to navigate ambiguity and remain resilient through change
  • When necessary, I’m able to introduce change into the organization and guide my team accordingly (Manager only)
Innovation
I try creative ways of doing things
  • I champion bold and productive approaches to my work
  • I set audacious goals, take risks, and share lessons learned
  • I create psychological safety for my team to promote experimentation and learning (Manager only)
Strategy 
(Manager and senior IC) 
I set direction and inspire others
  • I define the vision for my teams in alignment with company-wide priorities and inspire my team and cross-discipline stakeholders to work toward that vision
  • I generate excitement for my/the team's strategy

Talent

Attract, grow, and retain our people 
Responsibility
Key Behaviors 
Personal growth 
I’m curious and energized by learning
  • I proactively ask for feedback and know my strengths and development areas
  • I seek out opportunities to learn and grow
Hiring  
I build and retain a diverse team
  • I contribute to interviewing and hiring a diverse and talented team 
  • I attract and hire a diverse and talented team with a broad range of experiences and strengths (Manager only)
  • I actively work to engage and retain Dropboxers (Manager only)
Talent development
I contribute to building a high performing team
  • I offer honest feedback that’s delivered with empathy to help others learn and grow
  • I model integrity and a high standard of excellence for my work 
  • I provide real-time feedback and coaching to my team (Manager only)
  • I effectively delegate work based on my team’s skills, resources, and career goals (Manager only)
  • I hold my team accountable for delivering excellence (Manager only)
Organizational planning (Manager only)
I structure my team and build bench strength for the future
  • I structure my team so the right people are in the right roles with clearly defined responsibilities
  • I have a succession plan that sets my team up for success in the future

Culture

Role model collaboration and inclusivity 
Responsibility
Key Behaviors 
Collaboration
I partner and influence to reach the best outcome for Dropbox
 
  • I’m worthy of trust and lead by example
  • I help break down silos within and across functions and influence others to reach the best outcome for Dropbox
  • I avoid blame and cooperatively solve the right problems, disagreeing and committing when necessary
Organizational health
I contribute to building culture and a sense of belonging for Dropboxers
  • I motivate others to be their authentic selves everyday and contribute to building community
  • I support an environment where all Dropboxers are included and heard
  • I practice the Dropbox Diversity Commitments on a regular basis
Communication
I communicate clearly and share information proactively
  • I write and speak with clarity, tailor my message to my audience, and listen to understand others
  • I proactively share information so the right people are informed and aligned

Craft

Build and demonstrate expertise
Responsibility
Key Behaviors
Domain expertise
I’m skilled in my craft 
  • I demonstrate knowledge and expertise in my domain
  • I’m up-to-date on market trends and best practices within my domain
Business acumen
I understand what drives our business
  • I stay current on Dropbox’s business, product, and competitive market
  • I can explain Dropbox's company-wide goals and connect my work to them
Customer focus*
I deeply understand and delight my customers
  • I create experiences that delight my customer and meet their needs
  • I make decisions and adapt solutions based on insights about our customers
*Customers include internal stakeholders and external customers
 
Note: Craft is defined by functional teams in Career Frameworks. Functional teams customized Craft expectations as they saw fit.
